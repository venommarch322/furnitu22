Crispy Air Fryer Chicken

⏱ Prep: 15 min 🔥 Cook: 20 min ⏳ Total: 35 min 🍽 Serves: 4
[ Google Ad — In-Article ]

Air fryer chicken has taken the home cooking world by storm, and for very good reason. It delivers all the golden, crispy, crunchy satisfaction of traditional deep-fried chicken but with a fraction of the oil, calories, and mess. The air fryer circulates superheated air around the chicken at high speed, creating an incredibly crispy exterior while keeping the inside juicy and tender. Once you make chicken this way, you may never go back to deep frying again.

The secret to truly exceptional air fryer chicken is the buttermilk marinade. Soaking the chicken in buttermilk tenderizes the meat through its natural acidity while adding a subtle tangy flavor that pairs beautifully with the seasoned breadcrumb coating. Combined with smoked paprika, garlic powder, and a perfect blend of seasonings, this recipe produces chicken that rivals your favorite restaurant — right in your own kitchen with minimal effort and cleanup.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs or breasts, cut into even-sized pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk for marinating
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Cooking spray

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Marinate: Place the chicken pieces in a bowl and pour the buttermilk over them. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, or ideally overnight for the most tender results. The buttermilk breaks down proteins and infuses the meat with flavor.
  2. Prepare the coating: In a shallow dish, combine the panko breadcrumbs, flour, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly so the seasonings are evenly distributed throughout the coating.
  3. Bread the chicken: Remove each piece of chicken from the buttermilk, letting excess drip off. Press each piece firmly into the breadcrumb mixture, coating all sides generously. Press the crumbs on with your fingers so they really stick and create that ultra-crunchy layer.
  4. Preheat the air fryer: Set your air fryer to 400°F (200°C) and let it preheat for 3 minutes. This ensures the chicken starts cooking immediately and develops maximum crispiness from the first moment it hits the basket.
  5. Air fry: Arrange the breaded chicken pieces in a single layer in the air fryer basket, making sure they do not touch or overlap. Spray the tops lightly with cooking spray. Cook for 14 to 18 minutes, flipping each piece halfway through, until the coating is deeply golden brown and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Rest and serve: Let the chicken rest for 2 minutes on a wire rack to stay crispy on all sides. Serve with honey mustard, ranch dressing, or your favorite dipping sauce alongside coleslaw or fries.
💡 Pro Tips:
• Do not overcrowd the basket — cook in batches if needed for maximum crispiness.
• A light spray of oil on the breadcrumbs before cooking makes them extra golden.
• Use a meat thermometer to ensure the chicken reaches exactly 165°F internally.
• Try adding cayenne pepper or hot sauce to the buttermilk for a spicy Nashville-style version.
