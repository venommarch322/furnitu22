One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken

⏱ Prep: 10 min🔥 Cook: 40 min⏳ Total: 50 min🍽 Serves: 6
[ Google Ad — In-Article ]

This one-pan lemon garlic chicken is the ultimate weeknight dinner — it requires minimal preparation, uses just one baking sheet, and delivers the most incredibly juicy, flavorful chicken with crispy golden skin. The combination of bright lemon zest, fresh lemon juice, pungent garlic, and fragrant dried oregano creates a Mediterranean-inspired marinade that penetrates deep into the chicken for extraordinary flavor in every single bite.

What makes this recipe so appealing to busy home cooks is the simplicity. You mix together a quick marinade, rub it all over the chicken thighs, arrange everything on a sheet pan, and let the oven do all the hard work while you relax or prepare a simple side dish. The high oven temperature ensures the skin gets beautifully crispy and caramelized while the bone-in thighs stay incredibly moist and tender underneath. It is hands-off cooking at its absolute finest.

Ingredients

  • 6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large lemon — zest and juice
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Optional: baby potatoes or asparagus on the same pan

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat oven: Set your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a large sheet pan with aluminum foil or parchment paper for easy cleanup.
  2. Make the marinade: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, one teaspoon of salt, and half a teaspoon of pepper until well combined.
  3. Season the chicken: Pat the chicken thighs completely dry with paper towels — this is essential for crispy skin. Place them on the prepared sheet pan and rub the marinade generously all over every piece, making sure to get it under the skin as well for maximum flavor penetration.
  4. Roast: Arrange the thighs skin-side up with space between each piece. Roast for 35 to 40 minutes until the skin is deeply golden and crispy and the internal temperature reads 165°F (74°C) when checked with a meat thermometer in the thickest part of the thigh.
  5. Rest and serve: Remove from oven and let the chicken rest for 5 minutes. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and serve with rice, roasted vegetables, or a fresh green salad.
💡 Pro Tips:
• Pat chicken VERY dry before seasoning — moisture is the enemy of crispy skin.
• Add halved baby potatoes around the chicken for a complete one-pan meal.
• Use bone-in, skin-on thighs — they are juicier and more flavorful than breasts.
• Squeeze extra fresh lemon over the chicken just before serving for brightness.
